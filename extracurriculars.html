At the University of Michigan, I have been involved in many different clubs. I am extremely active in my business fraternity, which is Phi Beta Lambda (PBL). In PBL, I have held an officer position where I was Philanthropy Chair. As Philanthropy Chair, I organzied events to get the brotherhood more involved. I implemented new fundraisers by building connections with new charities and planned events that the bortherhood would enjoy, elicting the biggest positive impact. Challah for Hunger

Challah for Hunger created the Campus Hunger Project Cohort, which is a national leadership program for students to design and implement projects that engage their peers and communities. This was implemented because at least 3 million college students are food insecure. In my local chapter, I executed the social media presence to promote events where the proceeds raised go to Food Gatherers. I educated volunteers about food insecurity on college campuses and ways to help at U-M. I devised new events that would rebuild and expand volunteer participation.
