About Me

Hi, welcome to my Portfolio. My name is Sarah Stupak and I am a junior at the University of Michigan's School of Information. My focus is in Data Science and Analytics where I am learning to gather essential data to answer questions in a creative and conving way. I am passionate about the intersection of business and technology in order to create digital solutions that improve lives. Three words that I would use to describe myself are hard-working, motivated, and coachable.

A bit more about my background is that I am orginally from Ann Arbor, Michigan, but I now live in New Jersey. I have lived in four different states, including Michigan, California, New York, and New Jersey. By moving, I was able to gain a unique perspective on what it is like to be in different parts of the country. Often I get asked the question, "What was your favorite place to live?" I do not have a favorite place as they are all so different in the best way possible.

As a student, I am involved in many different organizations on my campus, which you will be able to access on my Extracurriculars page. In addition, I have had work experience in a few different industries, which has given me the opportunity to apply my skillset in multiple situations. Outside of school, I enjoy various activities, which you will be able to see on the Fun page. Thank you for browsing my portfolio!
