Fun

In my free time I enjoy doing many different activities. A few of the activities that I do daily are going on runs, cooking with my roommates, and playing with my dog. My roommates and I cook a variety of different meals. We make different types of salmon and chicken. On the side, we sometimes have cooked brussels sprouts with rice. Other nights, we will have a caprese salad and cauliflower.

In addition, I love to go to Michigan sporting events. My favorite sporting event is watching Michigan Basketball play. I am hoping that they will be really good this year. This year, I went to my first Michigan Hockey game against Winsconsin with my friends. I also go to all of the Michigan Football home games, which are always a lot of fun.

My favorite place is New York City because there is so much to do there. I love to walk in Central Park, try different restaurants in the city, and go to Broadway Shows. NYC has changed a lot because of COVID-19, but its unique character never will.

With my family and friends, I love to hang out and go to Yankees and Knicks games with them.
